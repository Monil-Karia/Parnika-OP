User Analysis

The analysis of user needs highlighted a major demand for easy-to-access sex education material. Data gathered from surveys and interviews showed:

  • 72% of users preferred mobile apps for learning sensitive topics.
  • 85% reported feeling embarrassed to seek in-person resources.
  • Users aged 15-25 were the most engaged with digital learning formats.
  • Key features requested: interactive quizzes, anonymous Q&A, and multimedia content.
